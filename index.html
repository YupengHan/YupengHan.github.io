Brief Biography

  • I am a research staff working at Robotics Institute, who is passionate about robotics and working with Prof. Maxim Likhachev. I am also actively seeking a Ph.D. position in 2021 Fall. Before coming to CMU, I obtained my master's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Purdue University and an undergraduate degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

Research Interest

  • I have broad interests in computer vision and artificial intelligence problems (such as object detection, pose estimation, scene understanding, etc.).

  • In addition to modifying neural networks to achieve good performance in new scenarios, I also implement computer vision in an analysis-by-synthesis manner. In the past year, I focus on exploiting GPU parallelization's advantage to solve outdoor scene object detection for autonomous driving and indoor object poses estimation for robot grasping.
